# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard 

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard built using Excel based on the Sales dataset.
The goal is to analyze sales performance, identify key trends, and provide actionable business insights.


## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze total sales performance across different regions
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Evaluate sales trends over time
* Compare performance across territories
* Enable dynamic exploration using interactive visuals


## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

*Excel
*Power Query
* Data Modeling (Star Schema)
* Pivot Tables


## 📂 Data Source

* Sales Database

  * DimProducts
  * DimTerritories
  * DimShipMethods
  * DimStatuses
  * DimDate
  * FactOrders


## 🧱 Data Modeling

* Star Schema Design
* Fact Table: Sales Orders
* Dimension Tables: Products, Territories, Dates, Status, Ship Method


## 📊 Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards (Total Sales, Tax, No. Customers, No. Orders)
* Sales by Territory
* Orders Per Category
* Sales Trend
* Top 5 Sold Products
* Orders by Status
* Orders by Ship Method
* Sales per Category


## 📈 Key Insights &  Recommendations

🟦 Territory Performance
Insight:Canada is the highest-performing region, while Australia shows the lowest performance.
Recommendation:
Focus marketing and retention strategies on Canada to maximize revenue
Analyze low-performing regions (e.g., Australia) and introduce targeted campaigns or pricing adjustments
🟦 Sales Trend Over Time
Insight:Sales grew from 2011 to 2013, followed by a noticeable decline in 2014.
Recommendation:
Implement recovery strategies such as promotions or product improvements

🟦 Product Category Performance
Insight:Bikes dominate total revenue, while Accessories contribute the least.
Recommendation:
Prioritize Bikes in marketing and inventory planning
Increase Accessories sales through bundling with Bikes 

🟦 Order Status
Insight:While most orders are completed successfully, there is a noticeable percentage of cancellations and rejections.
Recommendation:
Analyze cancellation reasons (delivery delays, pricing, product issues)
Improve customer experience and follow-up process
Optimize delivery timelines

🟦 Orders per Month (Seasonality)
Insight:Sales peak during May–June and drop significantly around September.
Recommendation:
Launch campaigns and discounts during low months

🟦 Shipping Method
Insight:The majority of orders rely on a single shipping method.
Recommendation:
Introduce alternative shipping options to reduce dependency

🟦 Top 5 Products
Insight:A small group of products generates the majority of total sales.
Recommendation:
Focus on promoting top-performing products
Ensure availability and optimize pricing for these products
